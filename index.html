Hi! I'm Lulu.

I am a third year Computer Science major at the University of California, Berkeley. I'm passionate about impactful technology as well as volunteering and engineering for social good! I aim to create thoughtful, well researched solutions to fulfill client needs. I am currently interested in backend development.

Most recently, I was a software development engineer intern at Amazon on the Amazon Device Management team, and prior, I was a software development intern at Generate Good. Outside of industry, I developed personalized coding curriculums for the students I taught at theCoderSchool, Berkeley.

Take a look at my resume or email me at lulu.yu@berkeley.edu.

Experience

  • Amazon

    I joined the Amazon Device Management team, which delivers secure and durable services to simplify how devices at Fulfillment Centers are managed and interacted with. I designed and implemented a command executor for Android devices used in Fulfillment Centers. My project provided device users and owners over-the-air command functionality on mobile devices and will support concurrent execution on ~590,000 managed devices.

  • Generate Good

    At Generate Good, I developed the platform’s database to support core functionalities including post dashboard and user creation. Over the semester, I implemented the front end of generategood.co’s user interface using HTML/CSS with Bootstrap. Additionally, I built a data analytics feature to generate impact reports for non-profit organizers and coordinators.

  • theCoderSchool

    I taught students the fundamentals of programming, problem solving, and algorithm design using a project based approach at theCoderSchool. I developed personalized curriculums tailored to individual learning goals and skill levels and guided students through building their own applications. I also ran week-long virtual coding boot camps where students created and presented a personalized project by the end of camp.

  • Retro Arcade Machine

    In April 2020, my friends and I built a 3x4.5 ft wooden retro arcade machine inspired by the vintage arcades of the 60's.
